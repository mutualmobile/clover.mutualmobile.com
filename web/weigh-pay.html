Weigh & Pay

Weigh & Pay enables your Clover Base Station to interface with a scale in order to sell items by weight.

Weigh & Pay Specifications

Platform:Android Base Station

Supported Scales:20 X 0.01 lb Portion Control Scale with RS-232C by CAS Corporation.

AppStore Link:clover.mutualmobile.com/mmdashboard

Email Support:weighandpay@mutualmobile.com

Considerations for Your Inventory

Set prices for weight based items per oz:

Currently only ounces is supported; support for more units coming soon If you choose a measure other than oz the “Unit OZ” at the bottom left corner will be greyed out and all units displayed on order will be automatically converted to oz.

Refrain from adding modifiers on weight based items

Modifiers will be ignored on weight based items in Weigh & Pay

Modifiers: only a single modifier can be added to an item

Consider setting up modifiers as products that can be added on to an order

Create Tare Weights (optional)

Creating a tare weight allows you to automatically subtract the weight of containers from your orders.

1. Tap “Edit Container” 2. All your items sold by weight will appear as container options 3. Tap the item you want to add a container for 4. Place the empty container on the scale 5. Tap “Add Container” 6. Give your container a name (e.g. Small) and tap the green check mark 7. Tap done

Using Weigh & Pay: The Main Screen

The main screen is set up with three sections: the scale read-out is on the left edge, your menu of items is in the center, and a ticket for an order is on the right

The Main Screen Scale Read Out

  1. The scale read-out updates automatically when items are placed onto or removed from the connected scale.

  2. If the scale is not connected, a red triangle appears at the top of the screen, over the top edge of the scale.

  3. If you don’t have anything on the scale and try to add weight items to your order, a red indicator will highlight the scale reading of 0.0.

  4. If you’re not connected to the network a red triangle will appear at the top of the screen.

The Main Screen Menu

  1. Categories in your menu are listed at the top of the menu section. The first category is ‘All’ shows all of your menu items.

  2. Switch categories by either tapping the category name or swiping on the screen.

  3. If you have more than three categories, there will be an arrow next to the third category. Tap the arrow to access the list of additional categories.

  4. Swiping on the screen will also navigate through any additional categories.

  5. Items are listed with items sold by weight first, then fixed price items.

The Main Screen Ticket

  1. The ticket lists the items added to the ticket, applicable tax and the total due.

  2. To add an item sold by weight to a ticket:

    1. Place the item on the scale. If the customer is buying multiple containers of the same product sold by weight, they can all be placed on the scale at the same time.
    2. Tap the item in the menu If containers are set up, select the menu item with the correct container size. If containers are used, and multiple containers are on the scale, tap the item with the correct container size the number of times that it is on the scale.
    3. The item will be added to the ticket section of the screen with the weight and price. If containers are used, the tare weight of the container(s) will be subtracted from the scale read-out.

  1. To add an item sold by weight to a ticket:

    1. Tap the item in the menu.
    2. If there is a modifier, then a list of modifiers will be shown. Tap the appropriate modifier.
    3. The item will be added to the ticket section of the screen with the quantity, price and any modification.
    4. If the same item (with the same modifier, if applicable) is tapped again, the quantity number will increment

Order Discounts:

To add a discount to an entire order:

  1. Tap ‘Order Options’ at the top of the ticket section
  2. In the list that appears, tap ‘Discounts’
  3. A list of discounts you offer will be shown. Tap the appropriate discount.
  4. The discount will be added towards the bottom of the ticket view.

Modify Item

  1. To add a discount on an individual item:

    1. Tap the item in the ticket
    2. In the list that appears, tap ‘Discounts’
    3. A list of discounts you offer will be shown. Tap the appropriate discount.
    4. The discount will be added underneath the item

  2. To remove an item from a ticket:

    1. Tap the item to be removed in the ticket
    2. In the list that appears, tap ‘Delete Item’
    3. The item will be removed from the ticket

Checkout:

  1. When a ticket is complete, and the customer is ready to pay, tap the ‘Pay Now’ button at the bottom of the ticket section.


  2. Tapping “Pay Now” takes you to the Clover’s main Register application to accept payment and complete the order. Once payment is complete, tap Done, and you will be switched back to the Weigh & Pay application to start a new order or pull up a recent order.

Delete or Save Order

  1. To delete an order, tap ‘Order Options’ then tap ‘Delete Order’. A confirmation will be shown, tap ‘Delete Order’ on the dialog to complete deletion.


  2. To save an in progress order, and start a new one, tap ‘ New Order’. Retrieve the saved order by going to Recent Orders.

Recent Orders

  1. Recent Orders shows your recent orders. The plus on the right can be used to create a new order. The most recently edited and unpaid order is second from the right. Moving to the left, the orders are unpaid and older followed by paid.

  2. Unpaid orders have a ‘Pay Now’ button at the bottom. Tap the ‘Pay Now’ button to go straight to payment. Tap anywhere else in the order to open the order in the Main Screen to edit the order.

  3. Tapping a paid order will open up the Clover Orders application. You can use Orders to reprint receipts, add tips, process refunds, exchanges and more.
