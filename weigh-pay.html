Weigh & Pay

Weigh & Pay enables your Clover Base Station to interface with a scale in order to sell items by weight.

About Weigh & Pay

Weigh & Pay is a native application for your Clover base station.

Install Weigh & Pay from the Clover app market today.

Before you begin using Weigh & Pay ensure your scale is correctly setup. Weigh & Pay supports the 20 x 0.01 lb Portion Control scale with RS232C by CAS corp. Follow the instructions accompanying your scale to set it up and connect it with your Clover base station.

Have questions about using Weigh & Pay? Check out our support page or email us.
