Weigh & Pay

Weigh & Pay enables your Clover Base Station to interface with a scale in order to sell items by weight.

About Weigh & Pay

Weigh & Pay is a native application for your Clover base station.

Install Weigh & Pay from the Clover app market today.

Before you begin using Weigh & Pay ensure your scale is correctly setup. Weigh and Pay requires you to use the CAS SW-RS (20LB) (US) scale — CLOVER CUSTOM BUNDLE. Please note that although this model is available from many retail sources, Clover's custom bundle is specially configured to work with the Clover station. Scales purchased outside of your Clover sales channel will not work with your Clover Station. Please contact your Clover salesperson to purchase. Additional details on the scale can be found here.
